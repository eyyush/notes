About the Author

Written by Ayushraj

This is my small plot of land in a quiet corner of Internet. I am a student-researcher with a variety of interest like computing, Indian classical music, video games, films, history, philosophy of subjects, etcetera. I got my first computer around 2010, which had x86-based Intel Pentium processor running windows 7 and a modest 2GB ram. As an introvert, i was naturally drawn to the world inside the screen- a place of curiosity and self-expression where i can be my true self without fear of being judged. I love to write and build things, because they give me a deep sense of meaning and identity, and helping me to make a sense of this life. I am a deeply nostalgic and traditional person. I hold a special fondness and nostalgia for Eastern European culture, the USSR, cold war and socialism. Since discovering Bitcoin, I have become interested in exploring various economic theories and about the human society. My tastes run counter to the culture- I like to watch arthouse films, and play retro games. I despise pop culture. My favourite genre is cyberpunk, and i like spy craft stories. I am passionate about journalism and learning the Russian language. I advocate for human rights, minimalism, slow movement, open source & free software, possibly non-violent direct action and Marxism.

For me, technology is a means to achieve these ends.
